# Diamond-Hands-PLC
This project is a lead qualifying automation workflow for a real estate and other business, with the sole focus on qualifying ; HOT, WARM &amp; COLD leads and respond approriately . This helps the business save time wasted on repetitive tasks and helps scale revenue.
# OBJECTIVE
General: To automate lead qualification, routing, communication, and team notifications for real estate companies, reducing manual work, improving response times, and enabling sales teams to focus on converting qualified prospects into customers.
This workflow reduces error in human lead handling, improves lead nurturing and conversion opportunities and this help drive revenue up by a faster and more consistent follow up.

# KEY FEATURES
Automated lead capture from Google Forms
Lead scoring and qualification logic
HOT/WARM/COLD lead segmentation
Google Sheets integration for lead tracking
Slack notifications for sales teams
Automated email follow-up sequences
No-code implementation using Make.com

# TECH STACK
Make.com (Workflow Automation)
Google Forms (Lead Collection)
Google Sheets (Lead Database)
Slack (Internal Notifications)
Email Service (Customer Follow-Up)
